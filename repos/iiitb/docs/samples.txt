IIITB is a post-graduate institution that opens its doors of higher education to those who have completed their undergraduate degree programmes and enables them to pursue Masters programme. The dual-degree integrated programmes provide an opportunity for motivated students who have completed Class 12 to set their sights on a Masters degree. 

All our M.Tech. programmes gear the students to face the corporate world in companies that are engaged in advanced R&D, products and services. The programme also provides a firm foundation for motivated students to pursue research by enrolling in PhD programmes in leading universities in India and abroad.
